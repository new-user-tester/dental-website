General Dentistry

Maintaining your oral health for a lifetime of smiles

Our general dentistry services form the foundation of good oral health. Regular check-ups and professional cleanings help prevent dental problems before they start, saving you time, money, and discomfort in the long run.

Our General Dentistry Services Include:
  • Comprehensive Dental Exams

    Regular examinations allow us to detect and address dental issues early, before they become more serious problems.

  • Professional Teeth Cleaning

    Our hygienists remove plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing and flossing can't reach, helping to prevent cavities and gum disease.

  • Digital X-rays

    We use low-radiation digital X-rays to detect issues that aren't visible during a visual examination.

  • Oral Cancer Screenings

    Early detection is key to successful treatment. We include oral cancer screenings as part of your regular check-up.

  • Dental Sealants

    Protective coatings that help prevent cavities in the grooves of your teeth where food and bacteria can get trapped.

  • Fluoride Treatments

    Strengthen tooth enamel and help prevent decay with professional fluoride applications.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile and boost your confidence

Our cosmetic dentistry services can help you achieve the beautiful, confident smile you've always wanted. Dr. Johnson combines artistic skill with advanced techniques to create natural-looking results that enhance your appearance while maintaining the health and function of your teeth.

Our Cosmetic Dentistry Services Include:
  • Teeth Whitening

    Professional-grade whitening treatments that can lighten your teeth by several shades, removing years of stains and discoloration.

  • Porcelain Veneers

    Thin shells of porcelain that are bonded to the front of your teeth to correct issues like chips, gaps, stains, or misalignments.

  • Dental Bonding

    A tooth-colored resin material that can repair chips, cracks, and gaps, improving the appearance of your smile in just one visit.

  • Smile Makeovers

    Comprehensive treatment plans that combine multiple cosmetic procedures to completely transform your smile.

  • Gum Contouring

    Reshape and recontour your gumline to create a more balanced, aesthetically pleasing smile.

Restorative Dentistry

Restore your smile's function and beauty

When teeth are damaged or missing, our restorative dentistry services can help restore both the function and appearance of your smile. We use high-quality materials and advanced techniques to create restorations that look natural and last for years.

Our Restorative Dentistry Services Include:
  • Tooth-Colored Fillings

    Natural-looking composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your teeth while effectively treating cavities.

  • Dental Crowns

    Custom-made caps that cover damaged or weakened teeth, restoring their strength, function, and appearance.

  • Dental Bridges

    Fixed prosthetic devices that "bridge" the gap created by one or more missing teeth, restoring your smile and bite.

  • Dental Implants

    The gold standard for tooth replacement, dental implants provide a permanent solution that looks, feels, and functions like natural teeth.

  • Dentures and Partial Dentures

    Removable appliances that replace multiple missing teeth, restoring your ability to eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

  • Root Canal Therapy

    Treatment to save severely infected or damaged teeth, relieving pain and preserving your natural tooth structure.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies can happen at any time. We provide prompt care for:

  • Severe toothaches
  • Chipped or broken teeth
  • Knocked-out teeth
  • Lost fillings or crowns
  • Dental abscess or infection
  • Injuries to the mouth or jaw
