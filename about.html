Dr. Emily Johnson

Specialist in Dental Care

Dr. Emily Johnson earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from New York University College of Dentistry, graduating with honors. With over 15 years of experience, she has established herself as a leading dentist in the community.

Dr. Johnson is committed to continuing education and stays up-to-date with the latest advancements in dental techniques and technology. She is a member of the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, and the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry.

Known for her gentle touch and warm personality, Dr. Johnson strives to make every dental visit a comfortable and positive experience for her patients. She takes the time to listen to her patients' concerns and works with them to develop treatment plans that meet their individual needs.

Our Philosophy

Patient-centered care that prioritizes your comfort and health

At SmileBright Dental, we believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality dental care in a comfortable, stress-free environment. Our approach is built on these key principles:

  • Preventive Focus: We emphasize preventive care to help you maintain optimal oral health and avoid more extensive treatments in the future.
  • Personalized Treatment: We recognize that each patient is unique and tailor our care to meet your specific needs and goals.
  • Open Communication: We take the time to listen to your concerns and thoroughly explain treatment options, empowering you to make informed decisions about your dental care.
  • Commitment to Excellence: We continuously invest in advanced technology and continuing education to provide you with the highest standard of care.
  • Compassionate Approach: We understand that dental visits can cause anxiety for many people, and we strive to create a supportive, comfortable experience for every patient.
